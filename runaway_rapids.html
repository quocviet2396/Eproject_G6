Runaway Rapids






Lazy River

Float down our lazy river all day long. Relax and soak up the sun.


Creek

Continuous fun awaits as you enter the crazy/lazy river. You can gently lull downstream the river in a tube or choose to play with all the interactive activities along the way. The rivers is 4ft deep at maximum height. Interaction is part of the fun on the river. Water detonators along the outside of the river release air bubbles to surprise tubers or cause a water explosion. There are also swinging fish to grab and waterfalls to go under!

Children under 42″ must wear a life jacket and be accompanied by a responsible adult. Riders must use single or double inflatable tube. Entry and exit on designated staired areas only.


Mountain Blast

Double your fun on this double tube and double wide slide. You can ride alone or with a partner but you will find it hard to control the spinning of the tube as you plunge down this rolling slide.

Under 48″ and non swimmers must wear a life jacket. Must be 42″ or more to ride. Maximum combined weight of 400 lbs.


White Slide #1

All attractions in the Power Tower section are geared primarily for children. The White slides are located in the Power Tower.

Body slide only. Tubes not allowed. 1 person at a time.


Interactive Play

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Children can get hands-on fun with water valves, sprayers, paddle wheels, water guns and more.



Water Mushroom

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Gentle waterfall structure that continuously spills out water.



Black Slide

All attractions in the Power Tower section are geared primarily for children. The Black slide is located in the Power Tower.

Children 16 and under. Must be 42″ or taller. No one over 200 lbs allowed. Must have a mat, not a body slide. 1 person at a time.



VooDoo Encounter

Twist and Splash! This is a pipestyle high-speed tube slide that bends and twists through an open and closed black tube.

Must be 42″ or taller. Under 48″ must wear life jacket. Combined weight of riders may not exceed 300 lbs. No single riders. All non-swimmers and novice swimmers are required to wear a life jacket.



Volcanic Revenge

Defy gravity on this uphill waterslide. After you and your partner plunge down hill, the speed propels you back uphill and then down again. What an experience! Under 42" cannot ride. All riders 42"-48" are required to wear a life jacket. New swimmers and novice swimmers must wear a life jacket regardless of height.



Nestea Plunge

Pretend you are Tarzan-type rope swing that allow guests to test their acrobatic skills under the watchful eye of a lifeguard.

Must be 48″ or taller. If you cannot swim or are a novice swimmer you must wear a life jacket. Single riders only. Splash down pool is 10 ft. Must be able to support own weight.



Soaring Thunder

Grab your mat and plunge head-first down this twisty and curving steep slide that sends riders quickly soaring and banking the sides as they speed down.

Must be 48" or taller to ride. Single riders only. Toboggan-style mat required.



Mega Bunga Falls

“Short and steep” is the idea behind this slide duo. Anyone who loves a thrill will enjoy the ride as they zip down feet first and drop into the splashdown pool.

Must be 42″ or taller. Under 48″ must wear a life jacket. Single riders only. Splash down pool in 10 ft. All non-swimmers and novice swimmers are required to wear a life jacket. Under 42" cannot ride.



Comfort Cove

Warming pool located beneath Mega Bunga Falls where you can take a short respite. Under 42" must wear a life jacket.



Magenta Slide

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Crawl tube allows children to crawl up or down.



White Slide #2

All attractions in the Power Tower section are geared primarily for children. The White slides are located in the Power Tower.

Body slide only. Tubes not allowed. 1 person at a time.



Lost Lagoon

Relax and enjoy yourself in this moderately heated spa pool before and after hitting the major attractions. Under 42" requires a life jacket. No infants allowed. Maximum of 30 minute time limit per person.



Dark Green Slide

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Offers the younger ones the thrills of our major attractions in a much smaller scale.



Moon Walk

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Try your skills on this obstacle course which requires you to hold onto the rope above you while balancing and walking across the moon pads. Must be able to reach the ropes.



Frog Slide

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Adorable slide designed specifically for our younger guests.



Side by Side Yellow Slides

Splash pool from 0″ to 12″ of water with all interactive water play. Short slides that sit next to each. Half the fun is climbing and playing your way to the top before sliding down.
